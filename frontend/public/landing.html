How it works

An entry in three steps

STEP 01

Photograph the receipt

Straight from your phone, as-is: crumpled, in the sun, handheld. Tricky photos are finished by cloud recognition — with zero setup.

STEP 02

Check it and add the mileage

Litres, price and total are already in the form. Add the odometer, mark «full tank» — and that's it.

STEP 03

Save — the rest is automatic

Consumption is recomputed, interval life updates, and when service comes due — Telegram reminds you.

The full-to-full method

Consumption is measured only between two full-tank fills — partial top-ups accumulate and don't skew the figure.

38.20 L ÷ 664 km × 100
= 5.75 L / 100 km
